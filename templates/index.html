Get Started

How to Use the Platform

Before you begin the magical experience, have a look at the instructions!

Once you enter the Meeting Room, please allow camera access from your device. MiTeC works best with Google Chrome and cookies enabled. You can then enter your name and click on "Join Call". Once in the call, you'll be prompted to allow Microphone access. Please do so. You can then explore the multiple features MiTeC offers and click on "Leave Call" when done. Hope you have an amazing time exploring!

Feature Break-Up

  • Mandatory Functionality
  • Adaptive Feature - Chat
  • Other Additions

As demanded by the Engage-2021 Team, a minimum of two participants should be able connect with each other using the product to have a video conversation.

Upto 50 Participants in the meeting

MiTec enables upto 50 participants to join the meeting and have a smooth video conferencing experience

Cross-Platform access

Although all the features work best on the web, MiTeC can be accessed and used through Laptops/Tablets and even your mobile phones!

An adaptive feature introduced during the challenege was the "Chat" Option. Here is how MiTeC enables chat!

Chat logs saved

Once you are in the meeting, you can Toggle the Chat button to see all the past chats of the room. The chats are available after you exit the meeting as well

Smooth texting experience

While in the meeting, you can send text messages seemlessly to other members of the room

To make the experience complete and wholesome, quite a few other features have been incorporated in MiTec

Screen Sharing

In a meeting and want to share a presentation? Fret not! MiTeC allows you to share your entire screen or one particular window

Reactions

A fan of emojis? The inbuilt reaction feature allows you to overlay your 5 favourite emoticons during the call

Active Speaker Detection

Wait, who was that? Aha! - MiTeC has a feature to detect the active speaker during a group call

Click to Zoom

Presentation too small on the screen? No worries! Click on the screen/person you want to zoom and there you go! You can always go back to the normal view by clicking again

Mute and Stop Video

Munching on some snacks but don't want your boss to notice? MiTeC has a solution! Just Mute and Stop your video for a while and continue to stay in the meeting
